What's in v1 (and what isn't):
  • Summary card: just two honest numbers — verified submissions + unique stations, plus "Aktywny od [date]". No fuel-coverage chips, no days-active count.
  • Rows: date + time on each, station name or Stacja nierozpoznana fallback. Verified rows show prices inline. Pending rows show a tiny italic W trakcie. Rejected rows just show a grey em-dash — no amber hint, no actionable copy.
  • Tap: matched row → map centred on the station; unmatched row → no-op.
  • Deferred (measure first, earn later): fuel-coverage chips, pending grace copy (>1h), actionable rejection hints, collapse/summarise failures. All revisit once admin/metrics/pipeline successRate1h tells us whether failure UX actually matters.

My read: Option A (amber hero) scores best overall and aligns most directly with the palette's intent — amber as the brand action colour. The palette note says "amber on a button/chip = brand action, not price" and a hero is the ultimate brand action surface. Option B is the safe choice if you want to preserve the light, functional feel of the map page across the whole site.

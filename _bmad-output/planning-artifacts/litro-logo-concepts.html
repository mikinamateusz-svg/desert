Litro · Logo Concepts

Four directions — each shown on light and dark background

Concept A — Pin
The i's dot becomes a location pin.
Map and fuel merged in one lettermark.
Pin accent doubles as app icon mark. Works at small size.
Concept B — Gauge
The o is a price gauge — needle on green
means this station is the cheap one.
Gauge arc mirrors the green→amber→red pin colour system.
Concept C — Measure
The L wears graduated price-scale marks —
precision and volume in one letter.
Scale marks echo the price pin spectrum. Very legible at small sizes.
Concept D — Nozzle
The t crossbar extends into a fuel nozzle —
the product is literally in the letterform.
Most literal concept — nozzle is unambiguous. Risk: overplayed in fuel apps.
